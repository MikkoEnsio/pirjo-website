    <p style="margin: 0 0 10px 0;">In my ceramics I strive for simple and bold forms and intense colors. My pieces are produced either by hand or cast in moulds in limited series. My ceramics is made of durable high-fired stoneware clays and the glazes I use are food safe. My career as a ceramist began with ceramic sculpture and later evolved to include utility articles and gifts, such as bowls and vases. I also sculpt custom-made cinerary urns to order.</p>

    <p style="margin: 0 0 10px 0;">In recent years my work has been increasingly motivated by themes related intimately to my own life, such as aging and grief. I'm trying to find a personal, three-dimensional shape of archetypal, shared emotional experiences. My artistic vision has broadened to encompass different materials.</p>

    <p style="margin: 0 0 10px 0;">I am increasingly working with artistic photography and exhibiting my photographs.</p>

    <p style="margin: 0;">The location of my previous studio Pot Viapori on the Sea Fortress Island called Suomenlinna was a continuous source of inspiration. Daily ferry trips to and from the island are always exceptionally beautiful and invoke strong sensations.</p>
