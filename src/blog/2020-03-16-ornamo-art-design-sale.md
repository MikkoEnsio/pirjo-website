I participated with my Bubblegum boys seria and two sculptures from What's Next seria in sales exhibition of unique Finnish art and design from 7 to 15 March 2020.

Organised annually by the Finnish Association of Designers Ornamo, the Art and Design Sale will bring hundreds of artworks to Helsinki Cable Factory, in March. These include unique objects, miniature sculptures, jewellery as well as ceramic, metal, glass and textile art by well-known and rising designers and artists.
