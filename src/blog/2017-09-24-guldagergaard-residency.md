This summer I spent two interesting months at Guldagergaard. I worked with theme "What Happened Next?" I made a three-dimensional diary on my feelings and emotions as an artist in residence.

## What's Next: Kyung Won Baek & Pirjo Pesonen 29 Sep - 9 Dec 2017

Can human feelings and moods be translated into clay and glaze? Absolutely. Kyung Won Baek (South Korea) & Pirjo Pesonen (Finland) show how emotions are expressed through ceramics.
